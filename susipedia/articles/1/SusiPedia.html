History

Early history

SusiPedia began as a project by famed writer and deity Cameron Impostor as a means to combat rampant political and academic misinformation on the internet in the mid 2020s. Spun-off from award-winning news site SusFeed, SusiPedia originally contained only articles pertaining to contemporary politics and media, but has since expanded to have over 7 million articles on a diverse array of topics.

Endorsement by Josh Groban and Ringo Starr

In its early days, Susipedia struggled to secure funding from its parent company. Following a mass media campaign by SusFeed executive Ringo Starr, Susipedia was able to recieve emdorsement and $5 million in private funding from both Josh Groban and Starr himself.
