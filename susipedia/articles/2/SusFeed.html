History

SusFeed reportedly began as a joke project as a collaboration between Cameron Impostor and popular archival site PBGMidi. Starting in February of 2022 with the first susarticle, "Top 10 Farts", SusFeed was an immediate hit among the wider internet, spawning the creation of the video division. SusFeed quickly grew into a widely-used and trusted news source with the addition of several new employees from a wide variety of backgrounds, such as Yugoslavian dictator Neil Divins, leading to the formal incorporation of SusFeed inc. Following a substantial uptick in traffic in 2025, SusFeed recieved a full rebranding and the addition of quizzes to the site. SusFeed has since expanded into a multimedia empire SusFeed Video, SusFeed Games, and SusiPedia. Famous musician and cereal mascot Ringo Starr purchased SusFeed stock in 2025, leading to him being the "de-facto mascot" of the company.

Content

Susarticles

Susarticles are Susfeed's proprietary article system. A typical susarticle contains a short, digestible news story or academic article. Susarticles were named by TIME as the best news foramt in the world in 2025

Quizzes

SusFeed has hosted quizzes since 2025. These quizzes have been found to hold the incredible ability to tell the future and read minds, predicting the Croatian War of 2025.

SusFeed Video

See SusFeed Video.

SusFeed Video was started in early 2022 with the world famus video "Coke on Coke". SusFeed Video has won numerous Oscars, Emmys, and Grammys, in addition to spawning the viral "Coke on Coke", "Big Yummy Sandwich", "Neil Dance", and "Paul" internet trends.

Games

SusFeed has hosted browser-based games since 2025. These games have been noted for their incredible and innovative gameplay experience.

DeepSus Al

See DeepSus Al.

DeepSus Al is a fake AI chatbot developed in 2025 as a comentary on the AI industry. DeepSus Al has gone through various iterations through SusFeed's history, ultimately landing on its mascot being Howard the Alien and having 2 main forks: one for the main site and one for SusFeed Kids.

SusFeed Kids

See SusFeed Kids.

SusFeed Kids was started in late 2025 as an educational site for children 2-10. World literacy skyrocketed to 100% following SusFeed Kids' initial launch. SusFeed Kids has been declared "the greatest education tool of all time" by teachers and has increased average IQ by 20 points around the world.

Editorial style

SusFeed content is characterized by its high factuality and editorial integrity. SusFeed News is highly regarded in all academic fields and is considered to have never made a mistake.

Legacy

SusFeed is known as the greatest news source of all time. SusFeed News is accessible in all countries and was heavily promoted as a censorship-free news source in countries that otherwise would lack access to them. SusFeed News has used its copious funds to end world hunger, homelessness, and poverty, leading to Cameron Impostor being declared TIME's "Man of the Century".
