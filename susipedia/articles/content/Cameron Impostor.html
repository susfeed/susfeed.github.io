Cameron Danger Hollabaugh (キャメロン・デーンジャー・ホーラボー, born before reality), known professionally as Cameron Impostor (キャメロン・インポスター), is a primordial deity, CEO, writer, game developer, linguist, politician, musician, videographer, and businessman known for creating the universe and, more importantly, SusFeed.com and The Boys and their various shenanigans.

Contents

Primordial existence

It is unknown how Cameron was created, since reality itself did not exist before him. It is assumed he, or at least his soul, has always existed in some form of existence. Little of what he did before the creation of this reality can be comprehended by mortal minds.

Creation of the universe

About 13.8 billion years ago, Cameron got quote "really bored" and decided to create the universe to entertain himself. Through the big bang, Cameron created the infinite multiverse and its composite universes, including ours: universe 1. This is the universe in which his physical form currently inhabits.

Early life

Cameron was born October 9, 2003, to lower-middle class parents. His mother divorced his father when he was young, because he was a cuck. Cameron had an interest in technology and language from an early age, and tried to learn about programming for the first time at age 5. He attempted to learn Mandarin Chinese at age 8, leading to the start of his interest in Asian languages.

Cameron left public school for online school during 5th grade, allowing him access to coursework that he would otherwise lack access to. He started to learn Japanese at Elizabethtown College at age 15, later skipping a year of high school to start full time sooner. Prior to his bachelor's, he made his first video game in 6502 assembly for the Nintendo Entertainment System.

University and The Boys

Cameron started university at age 17 as a Japanese and Computer Science major, later switching the latter to Information Systems. Through his academics, he met a group of rascals whom he made friends with. This group later formed The Boys, an international shenanigan creating organization. With The Boys, Cameron got the idea to create the famous site SusFeed from a series of parody article ideas intended to make fun of the news site BuzzFeed.

With the later expansion of SusFeed, the invention of susarticles, and the creation of SusFeed Video, Cameron used his influence to start researching the Ainu language from Hokkaido. This took some time away from SusFeed, leading to the great hiatus. Cameron came back for a full revamp of the site in 2025, remaining active online since.

Family

Cameron adopted his first son, Chip, from his abusive mother in late 2024, following him having custody for over a year. His second son, Piranha Plant, was adopted in mid-2025. He has a third son, though little is known about him at this time. Cameron also considers The Boys to be his family, especially as they have helped to raise his children.

Famed Kirby guy Neil Divins has expressed his support for Cameron even during the Smeff debate, angering much of the cuck community.
