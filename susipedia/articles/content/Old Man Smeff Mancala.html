John Smeff Mancala (born January 18, 5870 BCE), known professionally as Old Man Smeff Mancala, is an ancient figure from Mesopotamia known for his invention of the game mancala. Old Man Smeff has been present for most historical events, having a myriad of tales from all periods of history. Despite this, he is generally known for his tales from ancient Mesopotamia regarding the first game of mancala.

Contents

Life

Early life

John Smeff was born to lower-class parents in Mesopotamia in the early 6th millenium BCE. While ittle of his childhood is known, he had an interest in beads from childhood stemming from his parents' work in the mines. Smeff recieved no formal education for much of his life, gaining most of his knowledge from his many travels. His middle and last names, Smeff Mancala, was told to his parents by God, with God himself coming up with the name Smeff. The name mancala came from his divine destiny to create the game.

Invention of mancala

John Smeff invented the game of mancala sometime in his early 20s, initially making the board out of sand pits and using spare glass beads from his parents' shop. The first game of mancala was played shortly thereafter, leading to the most eventful game of mancala in human history. This game led to Old Man Smeff's catchphrase: "you know, 6,000 years ago, during the first game of mancala".

Unknown era

Little is known of Smeff's life following the first few games of mancala. What is known is that he was alive and active during all of history following his birth, leading to several conspiracy theories on what he was doing. The prevailing theory, hintend at by Smeff himself, is that he just kinda hung out for that time.

Current antics

Smeff reappeared in 2025 during the height od the Smeff Debate. Since then, he has been on various antics with The Boys, appearing randomly during shenanigans to impart his wisdom and impose chaos. To date, he has been involved in over 100 shenanigans.

Legacy

Joke of the Year

Old Man Smeff won Joke of the Year 2025 as the funniest person. This recognition has solidified him as one of the funniest people ever born.

Old Man Smeff's Joke of the Year icon.

Charitable work

Old Man Smeff works as the mascot for the Explain it With Smeff foundation, a prestigious educational foundation. Though this cause has garnered him much praise, it pales in comparison to his greatest charitable act: bestowing mancala to the world.
