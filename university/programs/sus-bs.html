BS in Sus Studies

Analyze, interpret, and master the art of being sus.

Program Overview

The BS in Sus Studies introduces students to the theoretical and applied dimensions of sus through foundational coursework such as Intro to Sus and Puff Theory. As students progress, they explore intermediate and advanced topics including The Sus Mindset: A Case Study and YouTube Poop Engineering, alongside broader studies in digital culture and meme systems. The program culminates in a capstone project demonstrating mastery of sus analysis and application.
