Program Overview

The SusFeed Certificate in Journalism provides a focused introduction to the field through core courses such as Intro to Journalism and Media Ethics. Students develop foundational skills in reporting, storytelling, and ethical media practices, preparing them for entry-level roles or further study in journalism and digital media.
