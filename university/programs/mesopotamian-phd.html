Program Overview

The PhD in Mesopotamian Studies is a research-focused program featuring advanced coursework such as Doctoral Mesopotamian Theory and Advanced Archaeological Methods. Students pursue specialized research including Doctoral Mancala Research while developing original contributions to the field. The program culminates in a doctoral dissertation.
