Program Overview

The Doctor of Sus (DoS) is a research-intensive program centered on advanced theoretical and applied exploration of sus. Core coursework includes Workshop in Sus and Loss Theory, where students engage with complex symbolic systems, abstraction, and layered meaning in digital communication.

Students pursue specialized research pathways, examining the evolution of sus as both a cultural and analytical framework. Emphasis is placed on original contributions to the field, including the study of transformative media, recursive humor structures, and the persistence of symbolic figures across platforms. The program culminates in a doctoral dissertation.
