BA in Mesopotamian Studies

Explore the origins of civilization through history, systems, and strategy.

Program Overview

The BA in Mesopotamian Studies introduces students to early civilizations through foundational courses such as Foundations of Mesopotamian Civilizations and Early Writing Systems. As students progress, they examine Ancient Societies and conduct deeper analysis in Mesopotamian Cultural Analysis. The program includes specialized pathways in strategic systems and early societal structures, culminating in a final BA thesis project.
