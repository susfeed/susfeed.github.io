BS in Smeff Studies

Explore the foundations, history, and applied systems of Smeff.

Program Overview

The BS in Smeff Studies introduces students to the field through foundational coursework such as Intro to Smeff and Applied Smeff Basics. Students build an understanding of Smeff as both a conceptual and practical system before progressing into historical analysis with Smeff: Early History and modern applications.

Advanced study includes Advanced Smeff and a culminating capstone experience, allowing students to synthesize theoretical and applied knowledge. The program prepares students to engage with Smeff across disciplines and real-world contexts.
