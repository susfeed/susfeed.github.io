Certificate in Puff Studies

Explore the origins, theory, and cultural impact of puff-based narratives.

Program Overview

The Certificate in Puff Studies introduces students to the foundations of puff theory through core courses such as Intro to Sus and Puff Theory. The program examines how puff-based symbolism has evolved within digital culture, media, and collective storytelling.

Particular emphasis is placed on the emergence of cereal-associated narratives and the role of reinterpreted public figures within these frameworks. Students explore how such narratives intersect with identity, humor, and recovery, forming a unique lens through which modern culture can be understood.
