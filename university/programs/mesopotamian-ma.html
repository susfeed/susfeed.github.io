Program Overview

The MA in Mesopotamian Studies builds on foundational knowledge through advanced coursework including Graduate Mesopotamian Studies and Smeff History in Mesopotamia. Students specialize through focused study such as Advanced Mancala Systems while developing independent research skills. The program culminates in a master's thesis.
