MS in Sus Studies

Examine sus through advanced theory, cultural narrative, and puff-based analysis.

Program Overview

The MS in Sus Studies builds on core foundations with advanced coursework such as History of Among Us and Advanced Puff Studies. Students critically examine the evolution of sus across digital culture, meme systems, and narrative frameworks. Special attention is given to the development of “puff theory” as a cultural construct, including its association with symbolic figures and media reinterpretation.

Duration

Concentrations

Applied Sus

Focus on real-world implementation through Applied Sus, analyzing behavior, communication patterns, and digital interaction in contemporary spaces.

Advanced Puff Studies

Examine the evolution of puff-related narratives through Ringo Starr: Life and Legacy, including the reinterpretation of public figures within cereal-based media and their role in reshaping identity, recovery narratives, and cultural mythology.
