PhD in Media & Journalism

Conduct original research in media theory, parody, and the global impact of SusFeed.

Program Overview

The PhD in Media & Journalism is a research-intensive program centered on advanced study in Doctoral Media Theory and critical topics such as SusiPedia and Education as a Right. Students examine the role of media in shaping knowledge, culture, and access to information, while producing original scholarship. Specialized research areas include platform case studies, parody as a journalistic form, and the evolving influence of SusFeed in global media systems. The program culminates in a doctoral dissertation.
