MA in Journalism

Advance your expertise in media systems, storytelling, and SusFeed production.

Program Overview

The MA in Journalism builds on foundational reporting skills with advanced coursework in Advanced Journalism Practice and Media Systems Analysis. Students explore modern media ecosystems, audience behavior, and platform-driven storytelling while developing professional-level work through SusFeed-focused production courses. The program culminates in an independent research project or thesis.
