The Radiohead Dilemma

We've all been there: trying to enjoy a Super Mario 64 song, trying to shop for ice cream, or even trying to enjoy a nice evening playing your favorite game. When suddenly, Radiohead.

Radiohead is currently one of the biggest threats to society, always appearing where you least expect it. Many believe it is an affliction that cannot be cured, that Thom Yorke will always be ingrained in your mind.

But do not fret, there is a cure! Just follow these simple steps to avoid Radiohead entirely:

Remain Calm

Remaining calm is the most important step. After all, if you panic, you can't complete the next steps.

Seek Shelter

Seek shelter, an abandoned house or cabon will work perfectly. Just remember, only find shelter away from any internet connection.

End It

There is no escape, Radiohead is all-reaching. Leave while you can.
