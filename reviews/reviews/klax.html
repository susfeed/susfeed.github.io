GAME REVIEW

Klax Still Delivers One of Arcade Gaming’s Most Addictive Experiences

A timeless arcade puzzle game that remains insanely addictive decades later.

10
Reviewed by SFGN • Arcade / Puzzle • 2026

It is 2026, and there is still time for Klax.

Few arcade puzzle games manage to stay relevant decades after release, but Klax somehow still feels fresh. The core loop remains incredibly satisfying: catch klaxs, match colors, survive.

Modern puzzle games often overload players with systems and mechanics, but Klax succeeds because of perfect simplicity. Every move feels readable and every klax feels earned.

Pros

  • Addictive gameplay loop
  • Interesting gimmick
  • Beautiful graphics
  • Klaxs are cool colors
  • Ported everywhere
  • Perfect convention downtime game

Cons

  • None. Klax is perfection.
FINAL VERDICT

Essential Arcade Gaming

Klax remains the cleanest and most replayable arcade puzzle game ever made.
